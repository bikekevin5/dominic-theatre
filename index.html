Asian and Queer? You're not alone.

Queer Asian Intersectionality doesn't come easy to a guy like Dominic, but he better learn fast if he's gonna to keep his relationships on track. San Francisco's new original play DOMINIC tells the story of what it means to be Asian in America, queer and Confuscian, and how to stay hyphy while collecting hongbaos. A journey through racial and sexual identities, DOMINIC is a look at what happens when Asian tradition and Western liberation collide. Workshopped and produced with special thanks to GAPA Theatre, DOMINIC plays May 10-12, 2019 at the Bindlestiff Studio Theatre as a part of the API Cultural Center's 22nd United States of Asian America Festival.
